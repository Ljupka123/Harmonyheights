Plucked and string instruments
From the gentle strum of a guitar to the rich hum of a cello, string instruments make every emotion sing. Whether you dream of rocking out, performing in an orchestra, or just learning your favorite song, this is where it begins! Join us and start plucking your first melody today!
  • Guitar
  • Harp
  • Double bass
  • Violin
  • Viola
  • Violoncello
Keyboard instruments
Pianos, keyboards, and more — the keys to endless creativity! From classical masterpieces to modern pop, keyboard instruments give you the power to play it all. Learn to express yourself one note at a time and bring harmony to every room. Hit the keys and start your musical journey with us!
  • Accordion
  • Harpsichord
  • Keyboard
  • Piano
Woodwind instruments
Flutes, clarinets, saxophones — the woodwinds are where melody meets magic. Their warm, breathy tones can be smooth and soothing or bright and bold. Discover how your breath can turn into beautiful music that tells a story. Sign up and let your first note take flight!
  • Bassoon
  • Clarinet
  • Oboe
  • Flute
  • Saxophone
Brass instruments
Ready to make some noise? Trumpets, trombones, and tubas bring the bold, brassy sound that fills concert halls and parades alike. Feel the power of the music vibrate through you as you play. Join our brass family and let your sound shine loud and proud!
  • Trumpet
  • Trombone
  • Tuba
  • Horn
Percussion instruments
Drums, tambourines, xylophones — percussion is the heartbeat of every band! Whether you like to keep the rhythm or steal the spotlight, we're waiting for you. Feel the beat, move your feet, and make some joyful noise. Sign up and discover your inner rhythm-maker!
  • Drums
  • Congas
  • Bongos
  • Timpani
  • Marimba
  • Bells
Voice
Your voice is the most personal instrument of all — and everyone has one! From pop to opera, singing helps you build confidence, control, and pure joy. Learn how to use your voice to share your story and connect with others through song. Take the mic and let your voice be heard!
